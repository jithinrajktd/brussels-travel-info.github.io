Privacy Policy

Introduction

Thank you for using the Brussels Travel Information Alexa Skill. Your privacy is important to us, and this document outlines how we handle your data.

Data Collection and Usage

This Alexa skill does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. The skill only uses your location, as provided by the Alexa service, to retrieve real-time public transportation information for the STIB network in Brussels.

Third-Party Services

This skill retrieves public transportation data from the STIB network's APIs. We do not control the data provided by STIB and recommend referring to their privacy policy for further information.

Limitations

This skill is designed exclusively for users in Brussels, and currently, no other transportation networks are supported.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
